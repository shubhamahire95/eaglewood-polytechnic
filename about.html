Who we are

Transforming aspirations into achievements

Venkateshwara Manav Vikas Mandal's Eaglewood Polytechnic Institute is a technical education institution at Sunanda Nagar, Phule Pimpalgaon, Majalgaon, District Beed 431131.

The institute is approved by AICTE, DTE and the Government of Maharashtra, and affiliated to MSBTE and Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (DBATU). Its diploma and degree programs combine academic fundamentals with practical training, industrial exposure, projects and mentoring.
