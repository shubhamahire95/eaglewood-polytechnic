Prospectus checklist

Documents required

Requirements depend on the admission pathway and category. Contact admissions to confirm your final checklist.

1

Core identity documents

  • School Leaving Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Domicile Certificate
  • Nationality Certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
  • CAP Allotment Letter
2

Academic documents

  • SSC marksheet and passing certificate
  • HSC marksheet and passing certificate, where applicable
  • ITI certificate / marksheet, where applicable
  • Diploma final-year marksheet and passing certificate
  • CET / JEE score card for FY Engineering
3

Category documents

  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Caste Validity Certificate
  • Non-Creamy Layer Certificate
  • EWS Certificate

The uploaded prospectus marks category documents as reserved-category or “if applicable.” Original requirements should be confirmed with the admissions office.
